The Artist

Meet The Creator

Rinku

Master Tattoo Artist & Founder

With over 8 years of professional tattooing experience, Rinku has mastered the art of transforming skin into canvas. Specializing in 3D realism, geometric mandalas, and custom designs, every session is a journey of artistic expression.

Trained in multiple tattoo styles from traditional blackwork to contemporary fine line, Rinku brings a unique fusion of precision and creativity to every piece. His work has earned 396+ five-star reviews and the trust of thousands of clients.
